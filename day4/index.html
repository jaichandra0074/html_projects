About Playground

GITAM Playground is an interactive platform designed for students to participate in sports, coding competitions, technical workshops, and cultural activities. The platform helps students improve their skills, teamwork, leadership qualities, and creativity.

Students can register for different events, explore activity schedules, and stay updated with the latest competitions happening in the campus.
